About LMG

Linus Media Group (LMG) is a Canadian technology-focused media company founded in 2013 by Linus Gabriel Sebastian.

Known for its popular YouTube channels like Linus Tech Tips, Techquickie, and Channel Super Fun, LMG provides in-depth reviews, tutorials, and insights on the latest in consumer technology.

With a commitment to delivering engaging and informative content, LMG has grown into a leading voice in the tech industry, amassing millions of followers worldwide and influencing tech enthusiasts and professionals alike.
